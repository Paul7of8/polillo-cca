About Photos

This page serves as a central collection of photos I have taken for Costa Catholic Academy. It is designed to give you easy access to images from past events and games — no more searching through old text message threads for links.

All photos are hosted on SmugMug and password-protected for privacy.

You will notice purchase options on SmugMug. This is a feature of the platform that remains enabled, though I want to clarify: I do not receive any income from purchases, nor is that my intent.

Found a photo you especially like? Full-size, original downloads are available directly through SmugMug. You are welcome to download, print, or share these photos with family and friends — whether directly or via social media.

None of my photos are good enough, but you may not upload these images to stock photography or similar commercial sites. They are not intended for resale, and this helps protect their personal use.

Enjoy!
